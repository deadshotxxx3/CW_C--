\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a 2D integer coordinate. 

Used for defining regions, lines, and points in image processing. Values may be negative during intermediate calculations but must be clamped to \mbox{[}0, width-\/1\mbox{]} and \mbox{[}0, height-\/1\mbox{]} before accessing pixels.
